Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how RedFlix IPTV ("the App") respects and protects your privacy.

1. Overview

RedFlix IPTV is a media player application that allows users to play their own IPTV (M3U) playlists. The application does not host, provide, or distribute any media content.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data such as name, email address, location, or identifiers.

All user data including playlists, favorite channels, and application settings are stored locally on the user’s device only. When the application is deleted or its data is cleared, this information is permanently removed.

3. Media Content and Responsibility

RedFlix IPTV functions solely as a video player. The legality and copyright status of the content viewed through the application depends entirely on the playlists added by the user.

The developer of the application cannot be held responsible for any content provided or streamed by third parties.

4. Third-Party Servers and Network Access

Streams accessed through the application are hosted on third-party servers. When connecting to these servers, your IP address may be visible to the content provider. This is an inherent characteristic of internet-based streaming and is beyond the control of the application.

5. Permissions

The application may request the following permissions:

  • Internet access: Required to load and play streams.
  • Storage access: Used only when necessary to cache playlist data locally.

6. Children’s Privacy

The application is not specifically directed toward children and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
